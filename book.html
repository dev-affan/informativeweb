Must-Read Books for Every Reade

Books transport us to new worlds and broaden our horizons. Here are some timeless classics and modern masterpieces:







1. "To Kill a Mockingbird"
( by Harper Lee )

"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee is a literary masterpiece
that delves into themes of racial injustice, moral growth, and empathy.
Set in the Deep South during the 1930s, it follows young Scout Finch's journey of understanding human complexities.







2. "1984"
(by George Orwell)

"1984" by George Orwell is a dystopian novel that
explores a totalitarian regime's oppressive control over society.
Through the story of Winston Smith, it highlights themes of
surveillance, propaganda, and the loss of individuality, presenting a chilling vision of the future.
